# Realm

A **realm** is one of the two fundamental domains that constitute the
universe in the P/A ontology. Each realm has its own internal structure,
admissibility conditions, and ordering relations. The universe at
iteration $n$ is the ordered pair

$$
U_n = (A_n, P_n),
$$

where:

- **Actuality $A$** is the realm of actualized events and their causal
  relations.
- **Possibility $P$** is the realm of admissible modal futures encoded as
  ray bundles and waypoints.

Realms are not spatial regions, subsystems, or Hilbert spaces. They are
structural domains that jointly define what *is* and what *could be*.

---

## Actuality

The realm of actuality contains:

- actualized events,
- spacetime locations of those events,
- and the per‑carrier causal chains linking them.

Actuality grows discretely: each iteration adds exactly one new event.
There is no global time parameter on $A$; temporal structure arises only
through the causal chains of individual carriers.

---

## Possibility

The realm of possibility contains:

- ray bundles,
- rays,
- waypoints,
- modal progression,
- and all admissible futures of every carrier.

Possibility is not constrained by locality, conservation, or causal
structure. It may contain mutually incompatible futures. Only pruning
and actualization impose physical consistency.

---

## Structural Role

The two realms provide the ontological separation between:

- the **modal** structure of admissible futures ($P$), and  
- the **actual** structure of realized events ($A$).

This separation is essential:

- Actuality determines which futures survive.
- Possibility determines which futures are available.
- Actualization selects one admissible interaction.
- Pruning removes incompatible modal futures.

Together, the realms define the dynamical evolution of the universe.

---

## Relations

- The **Universe** is the pair $(A, P)$.
- **Actualization** acts across realms: it selects from $P$ and adds to $A$.
- **Pruning** reshapes $P$ based on events in $A$.
- **Modal time** lives entirely in $P$.
- **Causal chains** live entirely in $A$.
- **Carriers** span both realms: they have ray bundles in $P$ and causal chains in $A$.
